My Sister Courtney

This was my first major film that I made on my own while at the Rochester Institute of Technology. I was challenged to step out of my comfort zone into the role of director. With picking the very personal topic of my sister for this film, I was able to more easily take on this directing role. The film uses various different forms of media including archival VHS footage, photographs, an interview with my mother, footage taken in the present day on a GoPro camera that I shipped to my family. I chose to edit this film using Avid Media Composer, a software I had only started using earlier in the semester. Rett Syndrome is a genetic disorder that is quite rare. Courtney Monk has had a severe form of this disorder for her entire life. Her parents, Richard and Lori Monk, have had Courtney live at home with them her whole life and remain as her caregivers. The COVID-19 pandemic has hit the Monk family particularly hard since Rett Syndrome causes immunocompromisation.
