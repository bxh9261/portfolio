Humanity Against Cards

This was the final of three short films that I was required to make for Production 2. I collaborated with two fellow students in the class that I had previously collaborated with for the first film for the class. Over the three day shoot, we were able to film all of our scenes and make time look as if it were passing more quickly. This was also the first time that we were allowed to use sync sound in a project which brought about more challenges and experience. I edited this film in Adobe Premiere Pro.

Fall 2018 - Production 1
Director, Cinematographer, Editor

Some people are more competitive than others when it comes to games, particularly when it comes to card games. When Cael is invited into a game of Cards Against Humanity with their friends, the heat of competition quickly gets the best of them, making for a chaotic game.
